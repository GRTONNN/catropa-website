A Brief History of Cats

Cats were domesticated around 9,000 years ago in the Near East. They were highly valued in Ancient Egypt, often associated with gods and royalty.

Origins of Domestic Cats

The domestic cat is believed to have descended from the African wildcat. Over centuries, they spread across the globe through human trade and migration.
